# Adidas Sales Performance Analysis

## 📖 Project Overview

This project analyzes Adidas sales data to uncover trends in revenue, profit, product performance, retailer performance, regional performance, and customer purchasing behavior. An interactive Excel dashboard was developed to provide actionable insights and support business decision-making.

> *Project Type:* Group Project (Subgroup Leader)

## 👩‍💼 My Contributions

- Analyzed customer purchasing behavior and sales channels using Microsoft Excel.
- Designed the interactive dashboard and presentation slides.
- Consolidated findings from team members into the final analysis.
- Presented the subgroup's analysis, which was selected as the- final submission for the group.

## 🎯 Business Problem

Although Adidas collects sales data across multiple retailers, products, and regions, the company requires meaningful insights to improve sales performance, optimize inventory allocation, and better understand customer purchasing behavior.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Measure overall business performance.
- Identify top-performing products, retailers, and regions.
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior across sales channels.
- Evaluate sales and profit trends over time.
- Provide data-driven business recommendations.

## 📈 Key Insights

- In-store sales generated the highest revenue and profit.
- Online sales recorded the highest number of transactions and units sold.
- Men's Street Footwear generated the highest revenue and profit.
- The West region consistently outperformed other regions.
- West Gear was the highest-performing retailer.
- Revenue and profit increased significantly in 2021 compared to 2020.

## ✅ Recommendations

- Strengthen online customer engagement.
- Invest more in high-performing product categories.
- Improve marketing efforts in underperforming regions.
- Optimize inventory based on customer purchasing behavior.
- Build stronger partnerships with top-performing retailers.
